About

Who am I you ask? Good question. But, a better question to ask is: "What's this website all about?". First things first, I'm Terrence from Stuyvesant High School's Computer Science class, here to indulge you on the precious art of drinking coffee and achieving 100% chill. That is, getting high. Sort of. But, in a good way. This page will be dedicated to everything you should do in order to achieve a stree free environment while enjoying your cup of Joe.

Each subsection on the top of the page, will tell you about the food Anteiku Café serves and how you should enjoy them. Please note, these are my "style" of achieving a comfortable, stress-free rest.

Anteiku Café is the name of a cafe in an anime/manga that I really love. If you guessed it, it's Tokyo Ghoul. So, the food and beverages in here, will be based off of the food, techniques, etc. of the anime. I guess you can call this a fan-page.
