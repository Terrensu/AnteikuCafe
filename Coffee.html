Coffee

Americano

What better way to start off, than the American drink. It is made by diluting 1-2 shots of espresso with hot water. Then you add twice the amount of water. So if u added 30 ml of espresso, than you would add 60ml of hot water.

Cappuccino

The popular drink among many people. It is similar to a Latte, but Cappuccino has more foam and chocolate placed on top of the drink. To make the perfect Cappuccino, you have to add equal parts of steamed milk, foam milk, and esspresso (60 ml) each.

Latte

Fun Fact: Latte gets its name because it's filled with milk. Add 60ml shot of espresso with 300ml of steamed milk. Then add 2ml of foamed milk.

Macchiato

This simple drink requires 30ml of espresso and a dot of milk foam.

Mocha

A delectable drink for those who crave sweet coffee. Add 60ml of espress, 120ml of hot chocolate and whipped cream on top.

Café Au Lait

A drink for the fancy. Add 90ml of French press coffee, then 90ml of scalded milk
